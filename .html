Start: Printer Problem
↓
Is the printer powered ON?

Check cables & connections
No ↓
Turn ON printer
↓
Any error message?
Yes ↓
Fix error (paper jam, low ink)
No ↓
Check paper & toner
↓
Print test page

DONE ✅

Check printer queue
↓
Restart printer & computer
↓
Network printer?
Yes ↓
Check IP & connection
↓
Reconnect to network

Reinstall driver
↓
Test print again
↓
Still not working?
Yes ↓
